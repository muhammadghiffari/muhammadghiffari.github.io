About Me

My Journey

I am a passionate and forward-thinking Information Technology student at President University, majoring in Informatics. With a strong foundation in full-stack web development, I have successfully delivered impactful projects such as a Thesis Title Selection System with Real-Time Timer, a Point of Sale application for small businesses, and a professional landing page for the Center of e-Learning IMERI FKUI.

During my 6-month internship at IMERI FKUI, I merged technical development with public relations strategy by designing event landing pages, handling stakeholder communications, and producing documentation for institutional workshops. I received the Best Internship Performance Award (Predikat Sangat Baik) for my contributions.

My aspiration is to build scalable, innovative digital solutions that bridge the gap between technology and communication, creating meaningful impact in education and beyond.
